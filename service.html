Service

I am actively involved in the wider computer vision and camera networks research community through conference organization and conference committees participation.

Conference Organization

I am the general co-chair of Computer Robot Vision 2016, which will take place in Victoria in June 2016.

I also served as the general co-chair for Computer Robot Vision 2015, which took place in Halifax.

In the past, I co-organized Camera Networks and Wide-Area Scene Analysis workshops in 2011, 2012 and 2013; these workshops were co-located with IEEE Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition Conference (CVPR), which is a top computer vision conference.

  • WCNWASA 2013
  • WCNWASA 2012
  • WCNWASA 2011

Journal Issues

I have also served as guest editor for a number of journal issues. I am currently serving as a guest editor for special issues in Computer Vision and Image Understanding journal and IEEE Sensors. Previously, I have also guest edited a special issue in IEEE Journal on Emerging and Selected Topics in Circuits and Systems.
